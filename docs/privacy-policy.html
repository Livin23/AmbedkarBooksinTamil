This app is a Tamil-language e-reader for Dr. B.R. Ambedkar's book "Castes in India". We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Bookmarks & Reading Progress

Bookmarks and reading progress are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server.
